Quizzs management

Quickly create, edit or search a quizz with ergonomic interfaces.

Results

See the students results and their mistakes. You can even search or organize the results by name, score, student, date...

A scalable and modular application

The webapp implements REST webservices. That means that if you want, for example an Android application, you can create only the front-end and query the database easily
